While at college we were offered the opportunity to participate in the Fife College game jam where we had to work as a team and develop a game in 5 days. As I have some experience working in game dev as a hobby I decided that I would take part in the event and asked two of my classmates if they would be interested in taking part in the event as I wouldn't be able to work on the sound and music while also programming. We were later joined by two more members, both animators and 3D artists. This left me as the only developer which was a challenge, but one that I was willing to face. We managed to develop and release our game within the time frame and won the student choice award for our submission. This release was not a completed game and has a very simple gameplay loop and has some unadressed bugs but we were proud of our work for the timeframe we had.
